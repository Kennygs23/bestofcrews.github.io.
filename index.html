Hand off the keys

Netlify will show you four custom Name Server addresses. Go to the registrar where you bought your domain (like GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc.), look for your domain’s "DNS Settings / Nameservers", and replace whatever is there with the nameservers Netlify gave you.
